Face mask policy: All customers and employees are required to wear face coverings/masks over their nose and mouth upon entry. Once you are seated at your table, you may remove the mask to enjoy your delicious food and beer, but we ask that you please put it back on to visit the restroom, peruse in our merch shop, and exit.

Physical distancing: We’ve rearranged our space to create 6ft distance between tables and dining groups at all times. We will not be offering bar seating or open seating, and you will have to have a table to come in. Reservations are preferred at Miramar, while Little Italy is first come, first serve. When you visit either location, you’ll notice floor signs to assist in flow – please bear with us and adhere to distanced guidelines to protect our guests and team members.

Cleanliness and sanitation: Our restaurants have gone through deep cleanings and we’ve increased our already meticulous sanitation processes to reduce risk. Dedicated team members will be sanitizing counter surfaces, restrooms, door handles, and tables and chairs between every seating.

Team members: We will be conducting daily wellness and temperature checks with our staff. Any team member displaying signs of illness will be sent home.

Contactless ordering: We’ve implemented QR codes at every table, which are set to display our beer and food menu in real time. If you prefer contactless ordering, simply point your smartphone camera at the code and our beer and food menu will display immediately. You will able to order directly from the table and/or add beer and food items to a preexisting tab.

We cant wait to see you again!
